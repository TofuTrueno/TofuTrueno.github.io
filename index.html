Descriptions

Old Watch

This was a picture that I had taken in a photography class where the assignment was to take a pair of photos that show the exact same thing but are somewhat different. From that I did this pair of photos with a modern watch and a replica of a pocket watch and held them in the same location. I also took the photo with the intenet of focusing on the watch so that's why the background is blurry.

Modern Watch

The other half of the pair for my photography assignment but instead of a pocket watch its a modern day wrist watch in the same location. This photo would probably more like the other if I didn't wear the watch properly and decided to hold it in my hand like the pocket watch.

Night

This photo was taken around my second week during the photography course and it was to mess with iso. I took this in my neighborhood during the evening. A normal photo would show a regular sunset but in this photo I was using a high iso allowing more light into the image and it made the sky look much brighter at this time of day.

City

In the first week of my photography class we were given a simple assignment to go and take a few photos using some basics we learned. This photo was one of the few I took in downtown Seattle and I wanted to get some shots of the buildings there. My focus in this photo was the building reflecting off of the other and I was using a low iso on the camera at the time to get a better image when the sun was out and that created this blue color effect on the buildings.
